Dallas' Layered Salad


Ingredients
2 pounds bacon
1 head iceberg lettuce
2 cups celery
1 cup green onions, green part only
2 cups green bell pepper
2 (8 ounce) cans water chestnuts
1 (16 ounce) bag peas
2 cups mayonnaise
2 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup Parmesan cheese

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a
baking sheet with aluminum foil. Lay bacon strips in a
single layer on the sheet. Bake in the preheated oven until
browned and crisp, about 15 minutes. Drain bacon slices on
paper towels. Chop bacon when cooled. In a 10 1/2-inch
round, 4-inch-deep dish, layer in the following in order:
torn lettuce, celery, green onions, bell pepper, water
chestnuts, peas, mayonnaise. Spread mayonnaise evenly over
the peas. Sprinkle with sugar, salt, and pepper. Layer on
Parmesan cheese. Top with bacon. Refrigerate for a minimum
of 2 hours.
