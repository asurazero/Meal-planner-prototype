Easy Breakfast Tortilla Pizza


Ingredients
1/2 tablespoon butter, softened
1 (10-inch) tortilla
3 large eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup total assorted toppings

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Rub
butter along the bottom and sides of a 8 or 9 inch cake pan.
Press tortilla down into the buttered pan and against the
sides. Crack each egg next to each other on the tortilla.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper and add desired toppings
evenly on top. Bake in the preheated oven until egg whites
are set and tortilla has become lightly browned and toasted
along the edges and bottom, 13 to 15 minutes.
