Mississippi Mud Potatoes


Ingredients
2 1/2 cups diced potatoes
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bacon
1/2 cup onion
1/3 cup mayonnaise
2 garlic cloves
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1/4 cup green onions

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray an
8x8-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Set aside.
Combine potatoes, cheese, bacon, onion, mayonnaise, garlic,
salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Transfer to the prepared
dish. Bake until potatoes are fork tender, about 1 1/2
hours. Top with green onions.
