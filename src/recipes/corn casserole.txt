Ingredients

Cooking Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Stir corn,
flour, sugar, butter, salt, pimento peppers, eggs, bell
pepper, processed cheese, and milk together in a mixing
bowl, respectively; pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Bake
in preheated oven until the cheese is melted, about 45
minutes.
