Blackened Shrimp Tacos


Ingredients
1 (14 ounce) bag classic coleslaw mix
1/4 cup red onion
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 jalapeno
2 limes, juiced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 pound raw shrimp
1 1/2 teaspoons seasoning
1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
12 flour tortillas, warmed
8 ounces guacamole

Cooking Instructions

Place coleslaw mix, red onion. cilantro, jalapeno, lime
juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and cayenne pepper in a
large bowl. Stir until evenly combined. Set aside. In a
separate bowl combine shrimp, blackened seasoning, and the
remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil. Stir until evenly
combined. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add
shrimp mixture and cook until shrimp are bright pink on the
outside and the meat is opaque, about 6 minutes, stirring
halfway through. Divide coleslaw and shrimp between the
tortillas. Top each taco with a dollop of guacamole.
