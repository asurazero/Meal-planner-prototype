Jalapeno Popper Potato Casserole


Ingredients
1 (10.5- ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter, melted
3 cups sharp Cheddar cheese, divided
6 slices bacon bacon
1 cup pickled jalapeño peppers
1/4 cup green onions
1 (30- ounce) package shredded hash brown potatoes
1 jalapeno pepper, thinly sliced

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
(200 degrees C). Coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking
spray. Whisk together soup, cream cheese, sour cream, and
melted butter in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in 2 cups
shredded cheese, bacon, pickled peppers, and green onions.
Add hash browns and stir to combine. Spread mixture in the
prepared baking dish. Cover dish tightly with foil. Bake for
1 hour. Top with remaining 1 cup shredded cheese and fresh
jalapeño slices. Bake casserole until bubbly around the
edges and cheese on top is melted, about 5 minutes more. Let
stand 10 minutes before serving.
