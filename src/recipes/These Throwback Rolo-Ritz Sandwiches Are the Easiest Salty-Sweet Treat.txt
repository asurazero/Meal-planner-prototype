These Throwback Rolo-Ritz Sandwiches Are the Easiest Salty-Sweet Treat


Ingredients
24 ritz crackers
12 Rolo candies

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Place Ritz crackers face down on a
baking sheet. Place an unwrapped Rolo on top of each cookie.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 3 minutes,
just until the candy is soft. Place another Ritz cracker on
top of each Rolo and gently press to smash the soft Rolo.
