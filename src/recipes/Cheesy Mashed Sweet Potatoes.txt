Cheesy Mashed Sweet Potatoes


Ingredients
1 3/4 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2-inch chunks
4 sage leaves
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup half and half
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup chopped pecan halves
1/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tablespoon chopped sage leaves, plus more for garnish

Cooking Instructions

Put sweet potatoes and sage leaves in a 2-quart baking dish;
top with 1/2 cup water. Microwave, covered, on High,
stirring halfway through, until potatoes are tender, about
10 minutes. Discard sage. Add Parmesan, half-and-half, salt,
and pepper. Mash with a potato masher until smooth. Heat oil
in a small skillet over medium heat. Toast pecans, panko,
and chopped sage, stirring frequently, about 3 minutes.
Sprinkle mixture over sweet potato mixture. Garnish with
additional sage leaves.
