Phyllis’s Braised Collard Greens


Ingredients
2 pounds smoked ham hocks
3 pounds collard greens - cl
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

Place smoked ham hocks in a large pot and add 4 cups of
water. Cover and simmer over low heat until meat nearly
pulls away from the bone, and the skin is a little tender,
about 1 hour. Remove ham hocks from the pot. Add collards
and red pepper flakes, and season with salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer until tender, about 1 hour. Add ham hocks
back in and cook for another 15 minutes.
