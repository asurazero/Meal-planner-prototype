Salmon Cream Cheese


Ingredients
8 ounces cream cheese,
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon dill
1 tablespoon capers
1 1/4 cups cooked salmon

Cooking Instructions

Beat cream cheese, lemon juice, salt, and pepper together in
a bowl with an electric mixer until light and creamy. Stir
dill and capers into whipped cream cheese and gently fold in
salmon. Refrigerate until ready to use.
