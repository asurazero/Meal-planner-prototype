Calico Beans


Ingredients
1 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup bacon, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can pork and beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can butter beans
1 (15 ounce) can lima beans, drained
1 cup packed brown sugar, or to taste
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup ketchup
3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon mustard powder

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
(175 degrees C). Cook ground beef and bacon in a large, deep
skillet over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain and
transfer meat to a 4-quart casserole dish. Add pork and
beans, kidney beans, butter beans, lima beans, brown sugar,
onion, celery, ketchup, vinegar, and dry mustard to the
casserole dish; mix well. Bake, covered, in the preheated
oven until bubbly and heated through, about 1 hour.
