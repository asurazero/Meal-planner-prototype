Caramel Apple Overnight Oats


Ingredients
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups apple cider
1 cup yogurt
3 tablespoons caramel sauce
1 tablespoon chia seeds
3/4 teaspoon apple pie spice
2 cups Granny Smith apples
M&Mâ€™S

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Stir together oats, apple cider,
yogurt, caramel sauce, chia seeds, apple pie spice, and 1
cup of the apples in a medium bowl until combined. Drizzle
caramel sauce inside 4 (12-oz.) jars. Spoon oat mixture
evenly into prepared jars (about 1 1/4 cups each). Cover
with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or up
to 4 days. When ready to serve, top each with 1/4 cup
remaining apples and drizzle with additional caramel sauce.
Top as desired.
