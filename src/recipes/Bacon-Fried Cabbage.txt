Bacon-Fried Cabbage


Ingredients
2 slices thick-cut bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 small cabbage, cored and sliced
1 medium onion, sliced
1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Cooking Instructions

Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until fat
starts to render, about 5 minutes. Stir in cabbage, onion,
caraway seeds, salt, and pepper. Cook, covered, stirring
occasionally, until cabbage is tender, about 15 minutes.
