Miso Soup


Ingredients
4 cups water
2 teaspoons dashi granules
3 tablespoons miso paste
1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Cooking Instructions

Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over
medium-high heat; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and
whisk in miso paste. Stir in tofu. Separate the layers of
green onions, and add them to the soup. Simmer gently for 2
to 3 minutes before serving.
