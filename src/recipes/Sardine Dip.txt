Sardine Dip


Ingredients
2 cans sardines in oil
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese,
1/4 cup mayonnaise
3 green onions, chopped, green and white parts
1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
1 teaspoon fish sauce
1 tablespoon seafood seasoning, (such as Old BayÂ® Original)

Cooking Instructions

Combine sardines, cream cheese, mayonnaise, green onions,
mustard, fish sauce, and Old Bay seasoning in a large mixing
bowl. Mix well by hand or with an electric mixer.
