Steak Burrito


Ingredients
1 (10-inch) flour tortilla
3/4 cup ribeye steak, warmed
1/4 cup exican rice, heated
1/4 cup pinto beans, heated
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon onion
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

Cooking Instructions

Fold a paper towel around tortilla. Microwave on High until
pliable, 5 to 10 seconds. Lay tortilla flat on a plate.
Layer steak, rice, beans, cheese, onion, and cilantro in a
line across the middle of the tortilla. Fold opposing edges
of the tortilla to overlap the filling. Roll 1 of the
opposing edges around the filling and tuck tortilla to form
a burrito.
