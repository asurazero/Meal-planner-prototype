Italian Style No Mayo Tuna Salad


Ingredients
1 15-ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1 medium tomato, seeded and diced
1/4 cup kalamata olives
1/4 cup red onion
1/4 cup chopped celery
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon capers
1 tablespoon chopped parsley
2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
2 (5-ounce) cans yellowfin tuna

Cooking Instructions

Combine cannellini beans, tomato, olives, red onion, celery,
olive oil, capers, parsley, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper,
garlic powder, and oregano in a large bowl; stir until mixed
together. Crumble tuna into bowl and toss to combine.
