Cooking Instructions

Mix thoroughly the coconut milk, cream-style corn, rice
flour, and sugar in a non-stick pot over medium heat. Stir
frequently while cooking until thick, about 30 minutes. Pour
into a serving platter with ridges, or a tray, and let cool
completely before serving.
