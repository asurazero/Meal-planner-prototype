Waldorf Salad


Ingredients
½ cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
⅛ teaspoon salt
3 apples — peeled, cored, and chopped
1 cup thinly sliced celery
½ cup chopped walnuts
½ cup raisins (Optional)

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon
juice, and salt together in a serving bowl. Stir in apples,
celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the
refrigerator until ready to serve.
