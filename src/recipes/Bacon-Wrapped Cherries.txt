Bacon-Wrapped Cherries


Ingredients
36 maraschino cherries, stemmed, drained and juice reserved
18 slices bacon slices, halved
36 toothpicks

Cooking Instructions

Set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and
preheat the oven's broiler. Wrap each cherry with a bacon
half, secure with a toothpick, and arrange on a baking
sheet. Drizzle reserved cherry juice over wrapped cherries.
Broil in the preheated oven until bacon is crisped to your
liking, 3 to 10 minutes.
