Watermelon Salad with Feta


Ingredients
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
3 cups arugula
1 cup sliced red onion
2 pounds watermelon, cubed
4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Cooking Instructions

Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and salt together in a large
mixing bowl. Add tomatoes, arugula, and red onion; toss to
coat. Gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
