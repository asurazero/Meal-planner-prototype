Broccoli Crunch Salad


Ingredients
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
salt to taste
4 cups broccoli florets
1 (12 ounce) bag broccoli slaw mix
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup red onion
1 cup bacon

Cooking Instructions

Combine mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, onion
powder, pepper, and salt in a bowl. Whisk together until
smooth and creamy. Set aside. In a large bowl combine
broccoli slaw, broccoli florets, red onion, sunflower seeds,
dried cranberries, and bacon. Pour dressing mix on top of
broccoli mix and toss until well coated. Chill until ready
to serve.
