Cooking Instructions

Mix orange cake mix and bake in a 9x13 inch cake pan
according to package directions. Let cake cool and poke
holes in cake 1 inch apart with a fork. Mix the gelatin with
the hot water and then add the cold water. Pour evenly over
the cooled cake. Combine the whipped topping, instant
vanilla pudding, milk, vanilla and orange extracts. Beat
until thoroughly mixed and thickened. Spread evenly over the
cake. Refrigerate cake for 1 hour before serving.
