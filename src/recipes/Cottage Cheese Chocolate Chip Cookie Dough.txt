Cottage Cheese Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough


Ingredients
1 cup 4% milkfat small curd cottage cheese
5 tablespoons maple syrup
3 tablespoons quick oats
1 tablespoon almond butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 pinch salt
1 cup almond flour
5 tablespoons semisweet chocolate chips

Cooking Instructions

Place cottage cheese, maple syrup, oats, almond butter,
vanilla, and salt into the cup of a high-speed blender.
Blend until ingredients are smooth. Add almond flour to
blender and blend until combined. Pulse if necessary to
prevent warming the mixture. Pour dough into a bowl and fold
in chocolate chips. Serve immediately, or cover and keep
stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
