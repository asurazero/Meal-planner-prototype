Spicy Creamy Cucumber Salad


Ingredients
2 English cucumbers
1 cup plain Greek yogurt
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons chili sauce
2 teaspoons ketchup
2 teaspoons hot sauce (such as Tabasco

Cooking Instructions

Place sliced cucumbers into a large bowl. Whisk Greek
yogurt, lime juice, Thai chili sauce, ketchup, and hot sauce
together in a small bowl until smooth; stir into sliced
cucumbers. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
