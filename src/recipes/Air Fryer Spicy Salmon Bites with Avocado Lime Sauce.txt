Air Fryer Spicy Salmon Bites with Avocado Lime Sauce


Ingredients
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon granulated garlic
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 ounce) skinless salmon filets, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/4 cup cottage cheese
1 avocado. peeled and pitted
1 garlic clove
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 lime zest
5 tablespoons lime juice

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions. Combine onion
powder, garlic powder, brown sugar, red pepper flakes and
sesame oil in a small bowl and mix. Rub salmon with spice
mixture on all sides. Spray the air fryer basket with oil.
Place salmon in the basket and cook in the preheated air
fryer until salmon flakes easily with a fork and is browned
all over, about 5 minutes, shaking halfway through cooking
time. Place cottage cheese, avocado, garlic, salt, lime
zest, and lime juice in the jar of a blender; blend on high
until sauce is smooth and well mixed, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve
salmon bites with avocado lime sauce.
