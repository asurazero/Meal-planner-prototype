Coconut Lime Fish Soup


Ingredients
1 1/2 pounds cod filets, or other firm white fish
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 lime, cut into wedges, or more as needed
2 tablespoons palm oil or olive oil
1 yellow onion, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper,
1/2 green bell pepper,
1/2 yellow bell pepper,
1 stalk celery, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons fresh ginger
1 tablespoon Old Bay
1 14.5-ounce) can roasted tomatoes
1/4 cup green onions,
1 (14-ounce) can coconut milk

Cooking Instructions

Pat fish dry with a paper towel. Season fish with salt and
pepper, squeeze lime juice over each fillet, and gently rub
in. Set aside. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat.
Add onions, red pepper, green pepper, yellow pepper, and
celery; cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, and Old
Bay seasoning. Cook until bell peppers begin to soften, 3 to
5 minutes. Add tomatoes and green onions, bring to a simmer,
and cook, uncovered, for 3 to 5 minutes. Place fish pieces
on top of the vegetables and pour coconut milk over the
mixture. Bring soup to a simmer, reduce heat to low, cover,
and simmer for 15 minutes. Taste; adjust seasoning with salt
and lime juice to taste.
