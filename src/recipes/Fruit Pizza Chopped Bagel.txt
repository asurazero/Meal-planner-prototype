Fruit Pizza Chopped Bagel


Ingredients
1 plain bagel, sliced in half horizontally
2 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon confectioner's sugar
1/2 cup mixed fruit,

Cooking Instructions

Toast bagel until golden brown, about 3 minutes. Meanwhile,
slightly spread cream cheese on a cutting board. Add
powdered sugar and fruit and chop and mix until chunky and
well incorporated. Gather mixture together; scrape up with a
spatula or bench scraper. Spread mixture onto bottom of
toasted bagel and place top piece on top. Serve immediately.
