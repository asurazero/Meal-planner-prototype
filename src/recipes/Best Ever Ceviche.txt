Best Ever Ceviche


Ingredients
1/3 cup red bell pepper
1/3 cup red onion
2 jalapenos, minced
1/3 cup finely tomato
1/3 cup cucumber
1/2 mango, finely diced
1 pound sea bass, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2/3 cup lime juice
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
1 avocado, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
fresh tortilla chips for serving

Cooking Instructions

Combine red bell pepper, red onion, jalapenos, tomato,
cucumber, mango, sea bass, and lime juice in a bowl. Toss
well to coat and refrigerate for 12 minutes, stirring every
4 minutes. Stir in salt, oil, and cilantro until well
coated. Carefully fold in avocado and serve immediately with
tortilla chips or tostadas.
