Wet Wet Wings


Ingredients
2 pounds chicken wings and drummettes
1 tablespoon oil
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup Wet Wet sauce
1/2 bunch green onions, green parts only, sliced (optional) green onions, green parts only, sliced (optional)
1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Cooking Instructions

Toss wings in a bowl with oil, salt, and pepper. Transfer to
the air fryer basket. Air fry the wings at 400 degrees F
(200 degrees C) until cooked through and crisp, 20 to 25
minutes. While wings cook, prepare the wet wet sauce and set
aside. Pour wet wet sauce over wings and toss to until well
coated with sauce. Sprinkle with green onions and sesame
seeds if desired.
