Cooking Instructions

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al
dente, rinse under cold water and drain. In a large bowl,
combine tortellini, spinach, cheese, tomatoes, and olives.
Add enough salad dressing to coat. Toss and season with salt
and pepper.
