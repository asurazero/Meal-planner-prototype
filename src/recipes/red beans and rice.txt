Cooking Instructions

Place broth, water, beans, rice, onion, bell pepper, celery,
Creole seasoning, garlic, hot sauce, thyme, oregano, and bay
leaves in the bottom of a multi-functional pressure cooker
(such as Instant PotÂ®). Stir to combine. Close and lock the
lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's
instructions; set timer for 50 minutes. Allow 20 minutes for
pressure to build. Release pressure using the natural-
release method according to manufacturer's instructions,
about 15 minutes. Remove the lid, stir, and let stand for 10
minutes to thicken. Divide among serving dishes and garnish
with parsley.
