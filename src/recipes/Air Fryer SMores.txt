Air Fryer S’Mores


Ingredients
1 sleeve graham crackers
5 (1.5 ounce) chocolate candy bars
10 sâ€™more-sized marshmallows

Cooking Instructions

Preheat an air fryer to 380 degrees F (193 degrees C). Lay
out a total of 10 graham crackers. Do not break apart. Take
10 sâ€™more size marshmallows and cut them in half
horizontally with a serrated knife. Place the sticky side of
each marshmallow half onto each square side of a graham
cracker rectangle. Place a piece of parchment into the air
fryer basket; place a graham cracker into the basket,
marshmallow side facing up. Air fry until marshmallow is
toasted, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove carefully. Gently break
marshmallow-topped graham cracker rectangle into 2 squares,
add a chocolate bar half on top of 1 square, and sandwich
the chocolate with the other square.
