Shortbread Crust


Ingredients
1 cup butter
½ cup confectioners' sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
¼ teaspoon baking powder

Cooking Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large
bowl, cream butter and confectioners' sugar until light and
fluffy. Stir together flour and baking powder; blend into
butter mixture. Pat into a 9 inch pie plate. Bake in
preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until edges are
lightly browned.
