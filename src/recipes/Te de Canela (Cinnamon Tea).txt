Te de Canela (Cinnamon Tea)


Ingredients
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 sticks cinnamon, broken into pieces
sweetener, such as honey, sugar, or piloncillo (optional)
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice, or as needed (optional)

Cooking Instructions

Place water and cinnamon sticks in a pot and bring to a
boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Strain tea and
pour into a cup; add sweetener and lemon juice as desired.
