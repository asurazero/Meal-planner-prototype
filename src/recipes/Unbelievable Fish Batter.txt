Unbelievable Fish Batter


Ingredients
1 cup all-purpose flour
½ cup milk
½ cup water
¼ baking powder
1 teaspoon salt

Cooking Instructions

Steps to Make It Gather ingredients. Whisk flour, milk,
water, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl until
smooth. To Use: Heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees F
(185 degrees C) Dip fish fillets into the batter to coat.
Carefully lower fillets, one at a time, into the hot oil.
Fry several fillets at a time, turning once, until cooked
through and golden brown, about 2 minutes per side. Drain on
paper towels.
