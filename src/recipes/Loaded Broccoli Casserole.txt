Loaded Broccoli Casserole


Ingredients
8 cups broccoli florets
8 slices bacon, coarsely chopped
1 cup onion
1 (10-ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
30 crackers, coarsely crushed
2 tablespoons butter, melted

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175
degrees C). Lightly coat a 2-quart baking dish with nonstick
cooking spray. Cook broccoli in a large pot of boiling,
lightly salted water for 1 minute; drain. Rinse with cold
water; drain again and set aside. Cook bacon in a large
skillet over medium until crisp. Using a slotted spoon,
remove bacon to a paper towel lined plate. Crumble bacon
when cool. Add onion to the skillet with bacon drippings;
cook over medium about 5 minutes or until tender. Meanwhile,
combine soup , sour cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper
in a large bowl. Add broccoli, bacon, and cooked onions to
the soup mixture and stir to combine. Add cheese, stir.
Transfer broccoli mixture to prepared baking dish. Combine
crushed crackers and melted butter in a small bowl. Sprinkle
crumb mixture over casserole. Bake 45 minutes or until
bubbly and golden. Serve hot and enjoy!
