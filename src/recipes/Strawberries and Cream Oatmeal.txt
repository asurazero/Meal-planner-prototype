Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal


Ingredients
1 1/2 cups strawberries
1 cup milk
1 cup water
2 tablespoons brown sugar,
1 pinch salt
1 cup oatmeal
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions

Place 3/4 cup strawberries, milk, water, brown sugar, and
salt in a blender cup and blend until smooth. Place oatmeal
and strawberry mixture in a saucepan. Bring to a boil,
reduce the heat, add remaining strawberries, and simmer
until thick, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in
vanilla.
