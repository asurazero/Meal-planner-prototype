Feta and Sardine Flatbread


Ingredients
1 12 inch) pizza crust
2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon seasoning
1/4 red onion
12 pitted Kalamata olives
1 4.375 ounce) tin sardines packed in olive oil
1/2 cup feta cheese
1 tomato Roma tomato
1 small lemon
1/4 cup spinach leaves

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Place
pizza crust on a round pizza pan. Whisk olive oil and Greek
seasoning together in a small bowl. Brush mixture over the
entire pizza crust, including the edges. Divide onion,
olives, and sardines over pizza crust. Sprinkle on feta,
then scatter on chopped tomatoes. Bake in the preheated oven
for 15 minutes. Remove pizza from the oven; add lemon slices
and spinach leaves. Cut into slices and serve immediately.
