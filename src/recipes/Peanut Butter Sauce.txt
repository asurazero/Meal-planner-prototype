Peanut Butter Sauce


Ingredients
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt

Cooking Instructions

Heat peanut butter and sweetened condensed milk in a
saucepan over medium-low heat, stirring until well
incorporated and smooth. Stir in milk. Remove from heat and
stir in vanilla and salt.
