Air Fryer Baked Yams


Ingredients
1 yam
1/2 teaspoon olive oil

Cooking Instructions

Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Scrub
yam clean and dry with a paper towel. Rub all over with
olive oil. Place yam in the preheated air fryer, and cook
until tender when pierced with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
