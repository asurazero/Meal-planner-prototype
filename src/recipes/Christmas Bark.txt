Christmas Bark


Ingredients
cooking spray
8 ounces candy coating
8 ounces chocolate
1 tablespoon shortening
1/3 cup candy-coated chocolate pieces (such as M&M’s®)
1 tablespoon sprinkles

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Line a large baking sheet with heavy
foil; grease foil with cooking spray. Combine candy coating,
chocolate, and shortening in a large microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on High until chocolate is melted, stirring every
30 seconds, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Pour mixture onto the
prepared baking sheet; spread out evenly, 1/4 inch thick.
Sprinkle with candy pieces and sprinkles. Chill until firm,
about 30 minutes. Break candy into pieces.
