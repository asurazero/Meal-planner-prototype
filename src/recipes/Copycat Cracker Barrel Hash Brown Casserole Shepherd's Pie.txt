Copycat Cracker Barrel Hash Brown Casserole Shepherd's Pie


Ingredients
1/2 recipe Easy Pot Roast
1 cup frozen peas
1 (24 ounce) container mashed potatoes
1/2 recipe ash Brown Casserole
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons green onion

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Place pot
roast and gravy in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in peas
and cook until peas are heated through, 3 to 4 minutes,
stirring occasionally. Add mashed potatoes to a casserole
dish and press into the pan in an even layer. Place pot
roast and peas on top of potatoes. Add hash brown casserole
on top of pot roast and spread into a thin even layer. Top
with Cheddar cheese. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven
until golden brown around the edges and bubbly, about 35
minutes. Sprinkle with green onions and serve.
