Best Chicken Salad


Ingredients
½ cup blanched slivered almonds
½ cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon lemon juice
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
1 stalk celery, chopped

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast
over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully,
as they burn easily. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice,
and pepper in a medium bowl. Toss with chicken, toasted
almonds, and celery. Enjoy!
