Cooking Instructions

Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly
grease 12 muffin cups. In a large bowl, cream together
shortening and 1 cup sugar. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt, and stir into egg
mixture alternately with the milk. Fold in blueberries.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups and sprinkle with
sugar. Bake in preheat oven for 5 minutes. Reduce heat to
375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and bake for 30 minutes more,
until golden.
