Cooking Instructions

Place Tajin on a plate. Moisten the rim of a glass with a
lime wedge. Press the moistened rim into Tajin. Set aside.
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add tomato juice, tequila,
lime juice, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco, horseradish, and
celery seed. Shake until the outside of shaker is frosted;
strain into the prepared glass.
