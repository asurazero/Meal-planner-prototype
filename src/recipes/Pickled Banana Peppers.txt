Pickled Banana Peppers


Ingredients
10 banana peppers
1 cloves garlic
1 slices jalapenos
1 1/2 cups vinegar
1 cup water
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt

Cooking Instructions

Pack peppers, garlic, and jalapenos tightly in a glass jar.
Bring vinegar, water, and salt to a boil, stirring
occasionally, until salt is dissolved. Remove from heat and
let stand 10 minutes. Pour warm mixture over peppers and
seal jar. Refrigerate 12 to 24 hours before using and up to
4 weeks.
