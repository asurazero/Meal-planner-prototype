Delicious Egg Salad


Ingredients
8 large eggs
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup chopped green onion
1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
¼ teaspoon paprika
salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring
water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and
let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove
from hot water, cool, peel, and chop. Place chopped eggs in
a bowl; stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard. Season
with paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir and serve on your
favorite bread, crackers, or salad greens.
