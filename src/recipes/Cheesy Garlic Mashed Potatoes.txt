Cheesy Garlic Mashed Potatoes


Ingredients
6 large russet potatoes
1/4 cup butter
6 cloves garlic
3/4 cup Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup heavy cream
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon green onions

Cooking Instructions

Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until
tender, about 20 minutes. Drain; mash potatoes with a potato
masher. Place butter and garlic in a large microwavable
bowl. Microwave for 30 second intervals until the butter is
melted and garlic becomes fragrant. Stir into mashed
potatoes. Add cheese, sour cream, heavy whipping cream,
salt, and pepper. Garnish with green onions.
