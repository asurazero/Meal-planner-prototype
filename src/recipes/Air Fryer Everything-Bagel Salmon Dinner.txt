Air Fryer Everything-Bagel Salmon Dinner


Ingredients
cooking spray
1 sweet potato, cut into 1/4-inch wedges
3 teaspoons olive oil, divided
1/4 teaspoon salt, divided
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, divided
1 tablespoon ijon mustard
1 tablespoon honey
2 teaspoons mayonnaise
2 cups broccoli florets
2 ounces) salmon fillets
2 teaspoons everything bagel seasoning
1 teaspoon soy sauce

Cooking Instructions

Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Coat a
sheet of foil with cooking spray. Toss sweet potatoes with 1
1/2 teaspoons oil and 1/8 teaspoon each salt and pepper in a
bowl. Arrange sweet potatoes in an even layer on the foil;
transfer to the air fryer basket. Cook until mostly tender,
about 10 minutes. Meanwhile, stir together mustard, honey,
and mayonnaise in a small bowl. In another bowl, toss
broccoli with remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons oil and 1/8 teaspoon
each salt and pepper. Push sweet potatoes to one side of the
basket. Add salmon to the other side. Spoon 1 teaspoon
mustard mixture onto each fillet; spread to coat tops of
fillets. Sprinkle evenly with bagel seasoning. Arrange
broccoli around sweet potatoes and salmon. Cook until salmon
is opaque and flakes easily with a fork and sweet potatoes
and broccoli are tender, 10 to 14 minutes. Stir soy sauce
into the remaining mustard mixture; drizzle over sweet
potatoes and broccoli to serve.
