Cranberry Brie Pull Apart Bread


Ingredients
1 ( 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 sourdough loaf or crusty Italian bread, unsliced
5 tablespoons butter, melted
2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoons fresh rosemary
1 teaspoon fresh thyme
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 8-ounce rie cheese, cut lengthwise in 1-inch strips, then thinly sliced crosswise
1/3 cup dried cranberries

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175
degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil. Cut bread with a
serrated knife from the top nearly to the bottom in diagonal
1-inch diamonds (do not cut through). Combine melted butter,
honey, rosemary, thyme, pepper, and salt in a small bowl.
Gently pull bread open at cuts and evenly drizzle with half
of the butter mixture. Stuff Brie and cranberries into the
cuts as you go. Place loaf on prepared baking sheet and
drizzle with remaining butter mixture. Bake until the Brie
is melted and the bread is toasty, 20 to 25 minutes. Serve
immediately.
