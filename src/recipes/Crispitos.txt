Crispitos


Ingredients
1 vegetable oil, divided, for frying
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 packet taco seasoning
10 flour tortillas
1/4 cup salsa con queso, divided, or to taste (optional)
2 cups heddar cheese
toothpicks

Cooking Instructions

Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil to a skillet over medium heat.
Add onions and cook for 2 minutes. Add beef and taco
seasoning and cook until beef is brown and crumbly, about 5
minutes. Drain extra liquid from skillet. In a large skillet
heat oil over low heat until hot. Meanwhile, spread a
teaspoon of queso sauce onto a tortilla as "glue. Add 1/4
cup browned beef in the center and top with 2 tablespoons
cheese. Roll up tortilla and secure roll with a toothpick.
Repeat with remaining ingredients. Carefully place rolled up
tortillas into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, 2 to 3
minutes. Transfer to a wire rack with a slotted spoon. Serve
with salsa con questo and enjoy!
