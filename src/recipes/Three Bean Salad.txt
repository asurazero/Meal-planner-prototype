Three Bean Salad


Ingredients
1 (15 ounce) can green beans
1 pound wax beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 onion, sliced into thin rings
¾ cup white sugar, or to taste
⅔ cup distilled white vinegar
⅓ cup vegetable oil
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground black pepper
½ teaspoon celery seed

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Mix together green beans, wax beans,
kidney beans, onion, sugar, vinegar, vegetable oil, salt,
pepper, and celery seed. Chill in refrigerator for at least
12 hours. Enjoy!
