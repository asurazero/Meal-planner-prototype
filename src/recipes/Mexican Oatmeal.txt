Mexican Oatmeal


Ingredients
1 cup water
1 cinnamon stick
1/2 cup oats
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon piloncillo
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions

Bring water and cinnamon stick to a boil in a saucepan.
Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Add oats and cook for
1 minute. Add milk and cook until thickened, 1 to 2 minutes.
Remove from heat. Stir in piloncillo, cinnamon, and vanilla.
