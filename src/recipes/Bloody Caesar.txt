Bloody Caesar


Ingredients
1 lime wedge
1 tablespoon celery salt,
1 cup ice
2 fluid ounces vodka
4 dashes Worcestershire sauce
2 dashes Tabasco
8 fluid ounces Clamato
1 celery stick, plus other garnishes

Cooking Instructions

Sprinkle celery salt onto a plate. Moisten the rim of a
glass with lime wedge. Press the moistened rim into the
salt. Fill glass with ice. Add vodka, Worcestershire sauce,
and Tabasco sauce, then fill with Clamato juice and gently
stir. Sprinkle in a pinch of celery salt, then garnish with
lime wedge, celery stick and other preferred garnishes.
