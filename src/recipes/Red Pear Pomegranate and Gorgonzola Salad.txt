Red Pear Pomegranate and Gorgonzola Salad


Ingredients
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 pinch salt
1 pinch ground white pepper
6 cups romaine lettuce - torn, washed and dried
⅓ cup chopped toasted walnuts
¼ cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
1 red pear, cored and sliced
¼ cup pomegranate arils
1 green onion, chopped

Cooking Instructions

Combine apple cider vinegar, extra-virgin olive oil, honey,
Dijon mustard, salt, and white pepper in a small container
with a lid. Cover tightly and shake until well combined. Set
aside. Place lettuce in a salad bowl. Sprinkle with toasted
walnut pieces and cheese crumbles. Fan pear slices on the
top of the salad and sprinkle with pomegranate arils and
chopped scallion. Drizzle with vinaigrette and serve.
