Everything Bagel Grilled Cheese


Ingredients
1 everything bagel,
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 slice American cheese
1 slice rovolone cheese

Cooking Instructions

Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Slather 1/2 tablespoon
mayonnaise on each cut side of bagel. Place 1 bagel half,
mayonnaise side down, in the skillet. Place both slices of
cheese on top; then place second bagel half, cut side up, on
top of cheese. Press down on sandwich with a spatula, and
cook until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes. Flip sandwich over,
press down, and cook until browned, 2 to 4 minutes more.
