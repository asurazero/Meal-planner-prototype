California BLT Chopped Bagel


Ingredients
1 everything bagel
2 ounces chive and onion cream cheese, softened
3 slices bacon
2 leaves romaine lettuce
1 slice tomato
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 avocado, diced

Cooking Instructions

Toast bagel until golden brown, about 3 minutes. Meanwhile,
slightly spread cream cheese on a cutting board. Add bacon,
lettuce, and tomato on top. Sprinkle with salt and pepper,
and chop mixture until chunky and well incorporated. Add
avocado and chop 5 to 7 more times. Gather mixture together;
scrape up with a spatula or bench scraper. Spread mixture
onto bottom of toasted bagel and place top piece on top.
Serve immediately.
