Cooking Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place
broccoli florets in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer shredded
chicken on top; press down onto broccoli. Combine
mayonnaise, heavy cream, chicken soup base, dill, and pepper
in a bowl; mix well. Spread evenly over chicken and top with
Cheddar cheese. Grease a piece of aluminum foil with cooking
spray and cover baking dish with greased-side down. Bake
covered in the preheated oven, about 45 minutes. Remove
aluminum foil and bake until golden brown, about 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and let stand for 10 to 20 minutes before
serving.
