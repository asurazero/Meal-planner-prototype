Balsamic Strawberries


Ingredients
1 tablespoon butter
2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
¼ cup granular sucrolose sweetener (such as Splenda®)
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
4 scoops low-fat vanilla ice cream

Cooking Instructions

Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the
strawberry halves, sucrolose sweetener, and balsamic
vinegar. Cook until the strawberries are heated through and
darkened to a ruby red. Place scoops of ice cream into
dessert bowls or stemmed glasses. Spoon strawberries over
the ice cream and serve.
