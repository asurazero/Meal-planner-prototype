Loaded Bacon Ranch Mac and Cheese Casserole


Ingredients
2 boxes white Cheddar macaroni and cheese
4 ounces cream cheese
6 tablespoons butter
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
4 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tablespoon dry ranch dressing mix
2 cups cooked chicken
1 12 ounce) package broccoli florets
8 slices bacon
1/2 cup green onions
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly
grease a casserole dish. Bring a large pot of salted water
to a boil. Add macaroni and cook just until al dente, 5 to 6
minutes. Drain; return macaroni to the pot. Stir in cream
cheese, 4 tablespoons butter, milk, and powdered cheese
packets included in mac and cheese box until well combined.
Stir in sour cream, 3/4 cup Cheddar cheese, and ranch mix
until combined. Add in chicken, broccoli, and bacon. Reserve
2 tablespoons green onions and stir in remaining green
onions. Pour mixture into a lightly greased casserole dish
and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Melt remaining butter
and add to a small bowl with panko and pepper. Stir to
combine and sprinkle mixture evenly over casserole. Bake in
the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, 20 to
25 minutes. Sprinkle with reserved green onions.
