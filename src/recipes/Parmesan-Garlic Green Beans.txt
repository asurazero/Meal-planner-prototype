Parmesan-Garlic Green Beans


Ingredients
1 pound green beans
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 teaspoon minced garlic

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Arrange
green beans on a parchment paper-lined rimmed baking sheet.
Lightly coat with cooking spray. Sprinkle with black pepper;
toss to coat. Roast in the preheated oven until softened, 15
to 20 minutes. Add Parmesan, garlic, and crushed red pepper;
toss to coat. Roast until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
Sprinkle with additional cheese and crushed red pepper.
