Southwest Chicken Power Salad


Ingredients
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon garlic
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons Mexican crema
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 5-ounce) container mixed salad greens
1 cup cooked chicken
1 15-ounce) can
1 15-ounce) can corn
1/2 yellow bell pepper
1/2 red bell pepper
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup otija cheese
1 avocado
4 thin slices red onion
1 jalapeño,
2 tablespoons pumpkin seeds

Cooking Instructions

For vinaigrette, place cilantro, olive oil, lime juice,
honey, garlic, cumin, and cayenne in the bowl of a blender
or mini food processor and pulse several times until well
blended. For a creamy dressing, include the optional Mexican
crema. Season to taste with salt and pepper. For salad, line
a serving plate with mixed salad greens. Arrange chicken,
pinto beans, corn, yellow bell pepper, red bell pepper,
cherry tomatoes, cotija, avocado, and red onion in a
pleasing design on top of greens. Drizzle with vinaigrette
(alternatively, place all ingredients in a large salad bowl
and toss with vinaigrette). Garnish with pumpkin seeds and
jalapeño slices.
