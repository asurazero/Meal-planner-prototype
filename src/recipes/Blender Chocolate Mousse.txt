Blender Chocolate Mousse


Ingredients
1 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup confectioner's sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons espresso powder
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 pinch salt

Cooking Instructions

Combine heavy cream, cocoa powder, confectioner's sugar,
espresso powder, vanilla extract, and salt in a powerful
blender such as a Vitamix. Blend for 30 seconds. Chill at
least 1 hour to let flavors meld.
