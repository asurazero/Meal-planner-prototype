Basic Congee


Ingredients
7 cups stock
1 cup rice
1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
3 tablespoons ginger

Cooking Instructions

Add stock, rice, salt, and ginger to a large saucepan, and
bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally,
making sure rice does not stick to the bottom. Reduce the
heat to low; simmer for 1 hour.
