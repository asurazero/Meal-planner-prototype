Pizza Chicken Is Our New Favorite One-Pan Weeknight Dinner Casserole


Ingredients
1 (15-ounce) can can
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
1 teaspoon garlic powder, divided
1 teaspoon onion powder, divided
1 teaspoon talian seasoning, divided
1 1/2 pounds chicken breast, chicken breast,
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
3/4 cup cheese
1/4 cup pepperoni
1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives
1/4 cup green bell pepper

Cooking Instructions

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Spread
1/2 of the crushed tomatoes over the bottom of a 9x13-inch
baking dish. Add 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon garlic
powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, and 1/2 teaspoon Italian
seasoning, and stir to combine. Lay chicken on top of
tomatoes and season with the remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt and
pepper. Cover chicken with remaining crushed tomatoes then
drizzle with vinegar. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 teaspoon garlic
powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, and 1/2 teaspoon Italian
seasoning over everything, then cover with cheese. Top with
pepperoni, black olives, and peppers. Bake until cheese is
bubbling and melted and chicken has reached an internal
temperature of at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about
20 minutes.
