Oreo Cookie Pie Crust


Ingredients
30 chocolate creme-filled sandwich cookies, such as OREOÂ®
5 tablespoons butter, melted

Cooking Instructions

Gather all ingredients. Add cookies to a food processor and
grind into very fine crumbs. Pour crumbs into a bowl, then
pour in melted butter. Mix with a fork until thoroughly
combined. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch
springform pan.
