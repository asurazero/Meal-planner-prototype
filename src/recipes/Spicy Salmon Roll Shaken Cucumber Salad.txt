Spicy Salmon Roll Shaken Cucumber Salad


Ingredients
1 nglish cucumber, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon cream cheese, softened
2 teaspoons mayonnaise
2 teaspoons S S
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
1/4 cup red onion
2 teaspoons everything bagel seasoning
1/4 teaspoon monosodium glutamate, such as Accent
2 teaspoons rice vinegar
2 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
1/4 avocado, peeled and diced

Cooking Instructions

Combine cucumber, cream cheese, mayonnaise, Sriracha,
jalapeno, red onion, bagel seasoning, MSG, rice vinegar,
smoked salmon, and avocado in a plastic container fitted
with a lid or a large jar. Shake vigorously until everything
is evenly coated. Serve immediately.
