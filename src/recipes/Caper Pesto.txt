Caper Pesto


Ingredients
2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup pine nuts
1/4 cup green onions
1/4 cup fresh dill
3 tablespoons capers
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest
2 cloves smashed garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Cooking Instructions

Gather the ingredients. Process basil, Parmesan, oil, pine
nuts, green onions, dill, capers, juice, zest, garlic, salt,
and pepper in a food processor until nearly smooth. Chill in
an airtight container up to 1 week; freeze up to 3 months.
