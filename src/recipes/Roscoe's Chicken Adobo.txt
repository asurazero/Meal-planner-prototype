Roscoe's Chicken Adobo


Ingredients
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 whole chicken, chicken
4 cloves garlic
1 onion, chunked
5 leaf bay leaves
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup white vinegar
2 cups water
8 peppercorns
steamed rice for serving

Cooking Instructions

Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per
side. Add garlic, onion, bay leaves, brown sugar, soy sauce,
vinegar, water, and peppercorns. Stir and bring to a nice
simmer. Cover with lid and simmer over medium heat for 15
minutes. Remove the lid and simmer for another 15 minutes.
Serve with rice.
