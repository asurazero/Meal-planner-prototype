Spicy Dill Pickle Tuna Salad


Ingredients
2 1/2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon riracha
2 12 ounce) cans hunk white Albacore tuna in water
1 celery rib, chopped
1 small carrot
1 whole dill pickle
2 green onions

Cooking Instructions

Whisk mayonnaise and Sriracha together in a bowl. Add tuna,
celery, carrot, pickle, and green onions, and stir until
evenly combined.
